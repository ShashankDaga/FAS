About Us - Faculty Appraisal System

Welcome to the Faculty Appraisal System, a platform that showcases the outstanding scholarly achievements of our esteemed faculty members. This system serves as a comprehensive repository, highlighting the intellectual contributions and accomplishments of our faculty in various academic fields.

Our system covers a wide range of academic outputs, including:

  • Patents: Discover innovative inventions and groundbreaking research outcomes patented by our faculty members.
  • Publications: Explore a rich collection of academic publications, including articles, books, journals, and conference papers, authored by our faculty.

The Faculty Appraisal System offers a user-friendly interface, enabling easy navigation through our extensive database. Whether you are a member of our institute, a researcher, or an enthusiast, you'll find valuable insights and knowledge within our repository.

We invite you to explore this platform and celebrate the remarkable contributions of our faculty members!
